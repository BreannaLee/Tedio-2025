Typography System

Main Heading - Poppins Bold 18px
Secondary Heading - Poppins Bold 16px
Body text uses Lexend Regular at 14px. This creates a comfortable reading experience that's approachable and easy to scan. Perfect for informational content that needs to feel neutral and trustworthy.
Alternative body text at 14.5px - also Lexend Regular for slightly larger content areas.

Typography Rules

  • Headings: Poppins Bold (18px main, 16px secondary)
  • Body Text: Lexend Regular (14px or 14.5px)
  • Never bold body text - keep it clean and readable
  • Line height: 1.4-1.5 for optimal readability
